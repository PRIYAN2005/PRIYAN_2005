Terms & Conditions - Blood Donation Camp

1. Eligibility

To be eligible to donate blood, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Be at least 18 years old.
  • Weigh at least 50 kilograms (110 pounds).
  • Be in good health with no infectious diseases.
  • Not have donated blood in the past 8 weeks.

2. Consent

By donating blood, you consent to the collection, processing, and use of your blood for medical purposes.

3. Confidentiality

We will maintain the confidentiality of your personal and medical information.

4. Safety

Your safety is our priority. Our staff will ensure that the donation process is safe and hygienic.
